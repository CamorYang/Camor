Color for poem’s phonetic

Color for Books of Songs is a data visualization to express the phonetic aesthetic feelings of the Book of Songs chromatically. The writing of poems takes phonetics into consideration to make it expressed impressively for a better spread. So we try to express the Book of Songs in the way that fit today’s aesthetic

This project was generated from Microsoft Student Club, I worked independently to conduct the coding and design process to make this project.

2019.10-2019.11

Show the aesthetic in classic anthology in China again

The Book of Songs has been a traditional anthology in China. It records Chinese ancient people’s life, farming, wedding and funeral affairs by spreading euphonically between kids and adults.

traditional way that anthology spread the knowledge, which is difficult and mentally-costly for people to perceive.

The challenge

Reappear the aesthetic in a different way

The key for us to reappearing the aesthetic is to turn people’s perception into a different way. In the ancient time, people created the poems by considering the phonetic rhythm, rhyme and semantic. Due to limited education, only educated ones could get the meaning of poems and it also costs them attention and analysis.

As a result, we attempted to create the poems newly in order to:

Change their basic way to perceive the poems.

Map the phonetic into a sense for aesthetic perception.

My role

I researched on the aesthetic semantic of anthology in China by literature research

I researched different kinds of data visualization then selected and refined on the effects for better display effects.

I conducted the data crawling and data cleaning to generate the restructured data for the processing of ML. I refined on the model of CNN to map the phonetic data into the chromatic data for data visualization.

Literature research to find out how different factors are considered to create a poem

With the literature, I found some perspectives that make up of a character in a poem that give sense to its semantic.

Generation of a character

Word & Semantic

It is connected to the semantic when people perceive it. For example, “蒹葭” means reed in English and “芦苇” in modern Chinese which is more common in our daily life.

The discrepancy and semantic similarity between  different words

The shared radicals of “蒹葭” and “芦苇”

Phonetic 

Like the phonetic symbols in English, in China, the characters show the pronunciation in a slightly different way that is known as pinyin in China.

Character of “蒹葭”, the title of a poem in the book of songs

The pinyin, where different characters have different tones, is unique in Chinese culture to express the positive or negative intonation that indicates the feeling of a word or a character.

Technical challenges

Transference from phonetic to visual elements

With the basic exploration, I have a rough expectation that since my purpose was to find a instinct way to express its aesthetic, I could start from the basic senses, including vision, auditory sense, smell and so on.

Since the vision is the most prominent way for us to perceive the world and it is a totally different channel for perception, I tried to map the phonetic elements into the visual elements.

3 senses

mapping based on a large scale of perception data

As I have researched on the mapping process, I got to realize that to transfer the phonetic elements into the visual elements, I need to have a large scale of data to reach statistical stability by the regularization of the data. So I have to get enough data to support the fit of the ML model.

A  large number of people's perception for collection could be used as the big data for process

Deeper Insights

Visual effects is a complicatedly combined elements

Since we have decided to map the phonetic features into the visual elements, we have to define the elements that account for our expected concept. As a result, we researched the generative arts and digital humanities to get insights from it.

different generative arts for reference from openprocessing.com

After my research, I found that the generation of visual effects are combination of different elements including colors, basic elements, element construction and mapping regulations.

Connected, arranged elements 

According to the effects I have found , every generative art and effect is formed by some basic elements like the noise, line, piece of shape’s rotation, extension, topological deformation etc.

Dim, fluent combinations

The elements are connected to each other in a designed way. For example, the cylinder would flow in a curved way, and the line may be linking to others in a straight way.

Color distribution

Different color is linked to different elements. And the color set is in tune with the concept behind. Some color sets are limited to some certain colors or particular hues to show the expected beauty.

Co- Design for color collection

I did the crowdsourcing in Baidu’s platform to require people give their expected color for the displayed poem word by word in terms of the phonetic data.

The mechanism of crowdsourcing

Parameter fitting for CNN model

The color - phonetic aesthetic relationship was processed with AI to get a refined model to generate new color set for every poems to come. I have adjusted the model for several times to get the best backpropagation algorithm to fit acceptable color cases.

The mechanism of neural network for our transformation from the character into the BSH value of the color

Generative color set

After the parameters have reached good performance, evaluated by users, the AI model automatically generated color set for a specific poem

Transformation from the poem to the phonetic and corresponding generated color

Integrate the color with generative art

I selected and refined on the generation mechanism of the art effect from Openprocessing. After debug and testing, the order of generation of the element “line” was from the upper left to the lower right first, then back to the next top and followed the loop.

the flow and color generation mechanism of the expected effect

Therefore we nested the color graduation loop to give linear chromatic change to each element of line in tune with the line generation loop.

Linear change for the process of colors
