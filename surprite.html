Your Wonderful Article Title

by Jialuo Yang & Aosha LongJun 1, 2021 to Jun 20, 2021

Surprite is a handheld controller for MR environment. It is designed to make people interact with the virtual environment with natural behavior and avoid frequent mistakes from indigestible gestures

As one of the greatest invention in the last century, mouse has brought the digital data into a mdia people can see, preceive and communicate with. With the movement on a flat surface and the slgiht click, people change and interact with the icon, character or the object in the screen.

With the development of technology, the digital world around has transformed from the 2-dimension interface to the 3-dimension environment. It has been unclear yet how we move and manipulate the virtual artifact in the 3D environment. It’s about the interaction principle of the future and the way we talk with the next-generation digital world.

Something is wrong with XR manipulation of hands

Something is wrong with hand manipulation in MR environment. People mistakenly throw or use their device due to wrongly designed gesture that is anti-intuitive. This led to absence of universal way to interact with it.

As a result, we are looking for a more way to make people interact naturally. Such a device could be built according to human.

How would people hold a real object?

We did the behavior research to observe. We hold the light clay in our  hands as accustomed, then observe how we capture it, keep it and what manipulation we want to make.

Drawing from the light clay , we have found some insights on people's manipulation as follows:

Fingers’ Matching

During the process we hold it, our 5 fingers work collaboratively. With thumb on one side and the other 4 fingers on the opposite side, They act as interaction force to hold the object.

The manipulation after holding is unclear.

Move? Or throw? Or change fingers used ? Why are these instructions required?

We need to know how would they move their hand, which finger are used to manipulate on different instructions. Therefore, we conducted the literature review and secondary research.

With literature review on  165 papers of CHI on haptic interaction, we discovered some trends in present research and practice

Action and reaction

We know that to establish these manipulations, we must have a cycle that when we make an instruction, we can perceive the artifact’s activeness of reaction. Continuous perception makes us know it works and it requires high frequency perception and decision.

Haptic and Mechanic

Haptic cogniton could handle simple information, but it’s a fast-perceivable sense. To guarantee the perception from continuous change, people need to shift their perception from the visible virtual environment to the haptic perception.

We researched on the virtual environment . It shows that in mixed reality, beside 2D menu/navigation, we have 3D objects available on the ground or in the air. The manipulations are classified to manipulate the object just like the real world, or to turn on the functional menu/ properties. Once the menu/ info details are presented, click could be applied to make further instructions. It’s clear that Physical manipulation in the real world could be detailed as follows.

  • Holding & Manipulation

Holding and motion are the basic manipulation. With 2 manipulatins above, we move every object.

  • Throwing & Placement

Apart from holding & motion, throwing and placement makes the object released from hand, motionlessly or with high speed.

  • Complex Manipulations

More detailed manipulations, such as opening the box, weaving on clothes etc. are complicated and vary widely according to the specific structure and usage. These manipulations could be packaged as a function in the virtual environment that might be the main function or one of the functions of an object.

Human centered technology

To realize such manipulation, we need to define and select appropriate technology and engineering to realize it.

Every action represents the change that require perception from sensors or components, so we could find that:

Continuously varying Compression

When holding the object, people compress by press their fingers on the artifact. Intuitive behavior indicate continuously changing compression, and that is difficult for traditional mechanical button.

Continuous change on virtual object

Also, such continuous compression need being transmitted to virtual object for simulation on the object.

Perceive volume at the boundary of compression

Meanwhile, the border of compression is the volume perception, Compression requires limit by support of hard surface of some material.

Nothing but moving 

Acting as proxy of virtual object, the artifact’s movement and direction need capturing. When people move it, they do nothing but capture so extra buttons like mechanical buttons are redundant.

Throwing

In the real world, throwing is generated by people’s release during movement, with objects driven by the inertia to move toward the original tendency.

Confirm with Gentle point

Subconsciousness-driven preview suggests an easy gesture to act and manage it by a gentle point. And complicated instructions are combined with several gentle points on the menu to confirm.

Based on the gesture above, we considered and selected the most suitable technology and sensors that could give the corresponding feedback.

Flexible Glue 3d printing

Therefore, we proposed a flexible glue 3D printing to make the action could cause continuously alter the modelling.

Resine 3D printing

Thus, we proposed a resine 3D printing to provide required hardness. 

motor integration

linear motor

Gyroscope

Along with the movement, we capture the tendency with the aid of gyroscope, sensing both the moving direction and accelerated speed from triaxiality. To map the natural movement of artifact to the virtual object.

Arduino-Unity prototype

we made a prototype to make the self-made integration have control of the virtual environment in Unity. We base the communication on the Arduino and Unity to connect components’ alteration and object’s data. Along with technique, we integrate the technology successfully.
