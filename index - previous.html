I’m Jialuo Yang, a Master’s student of Digital Media in Georgia Institute of Technology. I’m currently working as the research assistant in Ubicomp Health and Wellness Lab, and the Digital World & Image Group. My interest lies on the Internet of People, specifically on how to transmit the experience, tacit knowledge and their perception between through interpersonal connection.

I used to do some research-oriented projects on tangible interaction, perceived affective reactions and trust on autonomous vehicles. Besides, as a user researcher, most of my works are devoted to seeking human-centered design through rigorous qualitative user research.

I’m interested in researches generally on emotion and cognition, including emotional AI, bio-sensing, affective computing. If you are interested in cooperation, feel free to email me!!
